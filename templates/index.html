Welcome to the AI Satellite Collision Avoidance System. This platform demonstrates real-time monitoring of satellites orbiting Earth, visualizing their positions in 3D, and predicting potential collisions with space debris or asteroids. When a satellite is at risk, the AI can automatically generate commands to adjust its orbit, preventing collisions and maintaining safe operations.

Explore the 3D simulation to see satellites revolving around Earth, access the dashboard for collision risk analysis, and learn more about individual satellites in the dedicated pages.
